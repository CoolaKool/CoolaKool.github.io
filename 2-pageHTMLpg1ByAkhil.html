Brief History of Foods

Pizza

Pizza is an amazing, world-wide delicacy that almost everyone knows about. It is an Italian food that evolved from similar dishes in the early 19th century. The word 'pizza' was documented in 997 in Gaeta and spread across parts of the world.

Salad

Salad is another well-known food that originated way back when, in the ancient times. Ancient Greeks and Romans layered and mixed vegetables, herbs, and other foods to make salad. The first salad dressings were vinegar and oil, used by the Babylonions long ago.

Veggie Burgers

Most burgers are not veggie burgers. The veggie burger requires a special pattie without any of the usual meat in it to be special. This pattie was developed in the 1980s, but there is no concrete evidence to who made it first. In around 1981, Paul Wenner supposedly developed the first veggie burger, but according to another source, Gregory Sams invented it in 1982.

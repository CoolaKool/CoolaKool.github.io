Favorite Vacation Spots

  2. U.S. (Nevada)

However, there are many places I would like to go to or visit in my lifetime. Only a few are included in the following:

  1. Australia
  2. Europe (Rome)
  3. Washington, D.C. (U.S.)
  4. Hawaii (U.S.)

Below, you will see a small paragraph of information about a few of the above places.

Hawaii

Hawaii is a lush, warm tropical island paradise located south of the U.S. It is a popular vacation spot for tourists and families alike, with palm trees, gentle ocean waves, and luxurious resorts. You can indulge in quite a few activities there, and it is a great place to spend time with family!

Rome

Rome is an archeological beauty! It is filled with old relics and buildings from the ancient times, including the all-famous Pantheon and Colosseum! The Roman Forum (middle) is a great place to hang out with friends and family and generally have a good time! Hear jaw-dropping legends, eat fabulous cuisines, and have a blast!

Washington D.C.

Washington, D.C. is probably the highlight of the United States of America! The White House, the abode of the president of the U.S., sits there. Other buildings like the Capitol building, the Washington Monument, and the Lincoln Memorial are all highlights of this fabulous place as well.
